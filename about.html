The 'Apfel Project' is an indie RPG with prerendered backgrounds being developed by Hirou about a girl named Apfel and a rat named Kase.

These two folks start a journey to seek what's hiding on the other side of the mountains that surround their land only to discover the secrets behind Apfel's existence...

I'm pretty sure that you will love my game if you like things like Fullmetal Alchemist, Final Fantasy VII, Adventure Time, Mario and Luigi RPGs, Homestuck and all types of rodents.
